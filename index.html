About me

Riley Goodman

Software Engineer, PHP/Laravel specialist

I'm a 26 year old software engineer, soon-to-be father and lover of life. My interest in programming was sparked in 10th grade computer class when I accidentally opened Chrome's developer tools and realized I could make the Google homepage say whatever I wanted. It was right then and there that I decided I wanted to be an HTML Engineer, making 6 figures fresh out of a 12 week bootcamp.

Just kidding. I also don't take life very seriously. But in actuality, that moment did eventually lead me to landing my first job as a web designer, and the rest is history. For years I accumulated lists of software ideas early in my career with no outlet to turn them into reality, until I was introduced to Laravel, and I quickly fell in love. I am technology agnostic - I will work with whatever my company needs, Laravel or not - but PHP/Laravel are without a doubt the strongest weapons in my toolbelt.

I have a beautiful wife, two annoying dogs, and a baby boy due in April! They are what gets me out of bed in the morning... sometimes multiple times per morning. I also have entirely too many hobbies, and probably always will. Some fun facts about me: my fastest time on a 3x3 Rubik's cube is 11.74s, I can and will beat you at arm wrestling, I beatbox and just got my own loopstation so I WILL be famous soon... I love to write (as you can see), I've been drumming since I was 6, I'm 3 inches taller than my dad, and this website is 100% black and white because my design weakspot is color theory.

Sometimes I wonder where my career would be if I had put all that time into my programming. I guess we'll never know. But hey, the skill of learning new skills certainly carries over into the world of software engineering, so at least there's that.
